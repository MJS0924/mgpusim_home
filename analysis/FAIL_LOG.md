## PHASE 6 Critical Finding: SuperDir Monotone Violation

| PHASE6-CRITICAL | superdirectory | im2col + matrixmultiplication | SuperDir has LOWER L2 miss rate than CD (better: 0.4105 vs 0.4148 for im2col; 0.3898 vs 0.4513 for MM) but is SLOWER (worse: 0.300ms vs 0.297ms for im2col; 1.162ms vs 0.986ms for MM).
